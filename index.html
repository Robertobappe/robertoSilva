GlobalWallets

International Transfer and Multi-Currency Platform: GlobalWallet is an open-source project inspired by Wise, designed to simplify cross-border money transfers. It aims to eliminate the predatory fees of traditional banks while providing a transparent foreign exchange (FX) experience. ...

The Problem
Traditional banks often hide fees within "retail" exchange rates, charge high transfer commissions, and take days to process remittances. Furthermore, using international credit cards abroad subjects users to high tax rates (such as the Brazilian IOF).

Key Differentiators
• Real Mid-Market Rate: Unlike conventional institutions, GlobalWallet integrates with financial market APIs to offer the mid-market exchange rate.
• Tax Efficiency (Reduced IOF): Designed to benefit from a 1.1% IOF rate, compared to the 4.38% typically applied to international credit cards.
• Multi-Currency Ecosystem: Digital wallet supporting over 40 currencies with instant balance conversion.
• Payment Institution (PI) Model: Architecture focused on facilitating transactions and managing payment accounts without the overhead of a full-scale bank.

Core Features
Transfer Simulator: Real-time calculation of fees and comparative savings.
Instant Conversion: Currency "swaps" within the user account.
Transaction History: Detailed reports of sent and received funds.
KYC (Know Your Customer): Basic identity verification workflow. see more
